# IEEE Representation

$$
x=(-1)^s*M*2^E \\
M=
\begin{cases}
1.frac,Normalized, \\
0.frac,Denormalized
\end{cases}\\
E=exp-bias
\left\{
\begin{align}
bias&=2^{k-1}-1 \\
k&=length(exp)
\end{align}
\right.
$$
